Spinning

Membership: Sports / All-in-one

Description: Spinning is for everyone! Spinning or Indoor cycling is cycling while riding a special stationary exercise bike with a weighted flywheel in a classroom setting. During this workout, you can adjust the wheel resistance up or down to your own level. Just level up or down the way you feel in combination with the rhythm of the music and different exercises. The spinning exercises include flat roads, runnings, climbs (seated or standing) and accelerations. Combinations of different exercises are also possible. The training effect is: cardio training, optimal fat burning, and the joy of a pleasant training program. Spinning is motivating, energetic and fun.
